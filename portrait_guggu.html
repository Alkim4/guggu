Happy Birthday, Guggu ✦
This portrait was made entirely from code no brushes, no filters, no editing software. Every single point of light you see is a star ★ placed by a program that read each pixel of the image, measured its color, and typed a star in that exact shade thousands of them, one by one, row by row, until your face appeared. What looks like a photo is really just stars, wall to wall.
